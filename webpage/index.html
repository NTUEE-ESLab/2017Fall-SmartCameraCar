Smart Camera Car

A small camera car that can follow your motion and shot video for you.

Motivation

If you want to make a third-person video of yourself, but there are no helpers, and you don't have a good device, like a drone, to do that for you, how will you get it done?
We want to build a device to help we shot high quality, smooth videos.
Then we built this Smart Camera Car, everybody can use it.

Main features

Flexible

Like a tripod, you can install whatever you want on it. Feel free to use your familiar, high resolution devices.

Smooth

You don't have to hold your heavy camera, let the car do it. Don't have to worry about the vibration while walking, just like you have a stabilizer.

Automaitc

Follow you as you wish. Don't even need a person to control it.

Easy use

Don't have to worry about controlling. It won't hurt anyone even if your a beginner.

Cheap

Much cheaper than a drone or something. You only need a Raspberry pi, an Arduino, some motors and your creativity. Everybody can afford it.

Creativity

What will your car look like depends on you. All you have to do is making sure it can move.

Appearance

Every rose has its thorn. This is just out car, you can make your own one.
